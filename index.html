INTRODUCTION

The proposed alphabetic principle makes reading and writing easy, allowing the reader to pronounce words from their spelling, and the writer to spell them from their sounds. The Yaggir Balochi alphabet and its rules are founded on this fundamental principle. The statements of purpose of the Yaggir Balochi script are given below:

  • To provide the Balochi language with a standard Latin-based script that is clear, simple, and consistent.
  • To make reading and writing of the Balochi language, readily accessible to most users, regardless of their national origin and/or education level.

Yaggir Balochi is a simple Latin-based alphabet uniquely designed to facilitate the reading and writing of the Balochi language. The Yaggir Balochi script combines the basic Latin alphabet plus a few modified letters, and a handful of common sense rules and recommendations, in order to best represent the sounds of Balochi language.
