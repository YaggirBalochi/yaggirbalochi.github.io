How To Contribute?

In order to contribute to this cause, there are numerous things that can be done. The road to standardization is long and filled with obstacles.

Use Yaggir in your personal blog when writing in Balochi. Use Yaggir as alphabet in your website or forum. Use Yaggir as the standard of your local magazine (if you are and editor or journalist). Inform others on the benefits of Yaggir and encourage the usage of it.

There are also other more simple, but effective methods: Use Yaggir when chatting or when commenting on Balochi blogs, websites or forums. Use Yaggir when sending text messages on mobile phones (sms). Use Yaggir when writing emails or regular letters. Use Yaggir when making notes.

If you use Yaggir in any public or general media, please let us know and we will publish a link
